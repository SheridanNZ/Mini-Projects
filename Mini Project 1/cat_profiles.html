Name: Sunny

Gender: Male

Location: Auckland

Sunny
Personality:

Sunny is a 2-year-old ginger and white short-haired beauty with a heart full of love to give! He’s fully desexed, wormed, and flea treated, so he’s all set to settle into his forever home. Sunny is a sweet and affectionate kitty who loves a good cuddle session, and he’ll happily curl up beside you for some quality bonding time. Though he gets along well with other cats, he can be a bit shy around dogs, so he’d prefer a peaceful home where he can feel comfortable and safe.

Backstory:

Sunny came to us at Meow & Furever after being found roaming alone by a kind-hearted person. Despite his rough start, he’s incredibly gentle and ready to find a loving family who will shower him with attention and care. If you're looking for a companion who loves snuggles and has a calm, friendly demeanor, Sunny might just be your purrfect match!

Name: Sugar

Age: 5

Gender: Male

Location: Whangarei

Sugar
Personality:

Meet Sugar! Sugar is a shy and gentle 5-year-old boy with a soft white coat and small dark patches. Despite his calm, timid nature, once he feels safe and comfortable, Sugar loves to sit by your side and relax while watching TV. He may take a little time to warm up, but with patience and understanding, you'll earn his trust and form a quiet, special bond.

Backstory:

Sugar has had a rough start to life and is now seeking the TLC he deserves. He’s missing his tail and has a unique white nose, which will require sunblock to protect him from the sun. Sugar is best suited for a quiet, indoor home where he can feel secure and loved, away from loud noises or busy environments. If you're looking for a patient and sweet companion who just needs a little extra care, Sugar could be the perfect addition to your family.
