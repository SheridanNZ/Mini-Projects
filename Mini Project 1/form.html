The Adoption Process

Our adoption process is straightforward and designed to ensure the best match between you and your new feline friend. Here are the steps:

  1. Browse Cat Profiles: Look through our available cats here and select the one you'd like to adopt
  2. Fill Out the Application: Complete the adoption form
  3. Review and Interview: We will review your application and reach out for an interview to ensure a good fit and arrange a 'meet and greet' session
  4. Adoption Finalisation: Once everything is approved, you'll be able to take your new furbaby home
